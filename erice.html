Welcome to Erice!

Erice is a beautiful medieval town perched on a hilltop overlooking the sea in northwest Sicily. There are plenty of things to see and do here, from exploring the narrow streets to visiting ancient castles and churches. In this guide, we'll give you some ideas for the top things to do in Erice.

To get started, click on one of the items in the list on the left to see more information and photos.

Visit the Castle of Venus

The Castle of Venus is a Norman fortress that was built on the site of a Temple of Venus. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in Erice, and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside and sea. Inside, you can see the remains of the temple and the castle's medieval architecture.

Visit the Castle of Venus

Explore the Streets of Erice

The streets of Erice are narrow and winding, lined with old stone houses and small shops selling local products. It's a joy to wander through the town and soak up the atmosphere, stopping for a coffee or gelato along the way.

Explore the Streets of Erice

Wander through the Gardens of Balio

The Gardens of Balio are a beautiful public park in the heart of Erice. They offer a peaceful retreat from the town's hustle and bustle, with a variety of trees, flowers, and fountains. The park also offers great views of the surrounding countryside.

Wander through the Gardens of Balio

Take a Cooking Class

Sicilian cuisine is known for its delicious flavors and fresh ingredients. Why not learn how to make some of the local dishes yourself? There are several cooking schools in Erice that offer classes in traditional Sicilian cooking, including pasta making and pizza baking.

Take a Cooking Class

Sample Local Wines

Erice is surrounded by vineyards that produce some of the best wines in Sicily. Many wineries in the area offer tastings and tours, where you can try a variety of red, white, and dessert wines, and learn about the local winemaking traditions.

Sample Local Wines

Visit the Mother Church of Erice

The Mother Church of Erice is a beautiful baroque church that dates back to the 14th century. It is located in the town's main square, and features stunning frescoes and sculptures. The church is also home to a famous statue of the Madonna and Child.

Visit the Mother Church of Erice

Climb the Torretta Pepoli

The Torretta Pepoli is a medieval tower that was built by the Normans in the 12th century. It is one of the highest points in Erice, and offers incredible views of the town and the surrounding area. You can climb to the top of the tower for a panoramic view of the landscape.

Enjoy Panoramic Views from the Torretta Pepoli

Learn about Sicilian Culture at the Pepoli Regional Museum

The Pepoli Regional Museum is a cultural museum located in the center of Erice. It features exhibits about Sicilian history, art, and culture, including ancient artifacts, paintings, and sculptures. The museum is housed in a former monastery, and its beautiful architecture is a must-see.

Learn about Sicilian Culture at the Pepoli Regional Museum

Take a Hike on Monte Cofano

Monte Cofano is a beautiful nature reserve located near Erice. It features stunning coastal views, rocky cliffs, and diverse flora and fauna. There are several hiking trails in the park, ranging from easy to challenging, so you can choose the one that suits your fitness level.

Take a Hike on Monte Cofano
